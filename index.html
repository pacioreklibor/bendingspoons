Hello, my name is Libor

About me

I live in a small town called Hlavnice near Opava in the Czech Republic. My three children are a 12 year old daughter, a 5 year old son and a 4 month old girl. Outside of work, I mostly hang out with my family. If time permits, I enjoy going cycling, going for a workout, or going to the sauna. As someone who enjoys socializing, I occasionally enjoy going out for a drink with friends. We love to travel a lot and enjoy it, often visiting the mountains in Czech Republic. We particularly enjoy the Mediterranean climate, culture, and cuisine. I am fascinated by the United States, which I have visited twice.

Profile

  • Over 15 years in the IT industry, I have worked on various projects such as automotive, industrial automation, CRM systems, finance, and development. My knowledge and learning opportunities in broad wide technologies were provided by project diversity and specificity.
  • The primary technology with which I collaborated comprised Microsoft products (Windows Server, MSSQL, Azure, GitHub, PowerShell), Oracle Database, and Oracle Cloud, for which I obtained a certificate (Oracle Cloud Infrastructure Foundations 2021 Associate), as well as a broad understanding of programming languages such as Python. Maintaining MSSQL servers, creating SQL queries, Functions, and Stored Procedures. Understanding of networking (VLAN, DNS, VPN, firewall, etc.)
  • Knowledge of cloud and development tools like Azure, OCI, Kubernetes, Docker, Terraform, Ansible, Azure DevOps CI/CD, and Git.
  • Working on top-notch documentation, education tools, and knowledge base creation. Excellent understanding of security requirements and privacy.
  • Team player who can engage in conversation with colleagues, provide help, and provide training. I'm capable of coming up with ideas and discussions using a pragmatic approach to problems. Experience working with people from all over the world, with extensive experience in working with them. Willing to learn new technologies all the time.

Motivation

I was delighted when I came across the job advertisement for a Software Engineer at Bending Spoons on LinkedIn.
After reading your article, I became interested in your company, which I honestly had not heard of. But actually, I've heard a lot about your products like Evernote and Meetup, which makes me even more interested.

I like what you believe in and what you want to achieve, we have similar goals. I am deeply concerned about the standard of the product that I am engaged in. The customer and every detail that can affect the comfort of using the product are important to me. I am not comfortable standing in the background, I try to get involved in the discussion. I am not solely interested in obtaining a salary and occupying a position, but rather in fostering and enhancing my skills, building a robust product that customers can rely on, and they like, and exchanging ideas and knowledge. Furthermore, I strive to achieve perfection in every aspect of my work, and of course, I accept personal accountability for my actions.

From the job description, you are looking for a person who can think logically and critically, who can accept the opinions of others, a person who wants to push things further. I worked as a technical support engineer for most of my professional career, but I have always been interested in software development. I have previously developed several Python or PowerShell scripts; however, I cannot claim to be an expert in these scripting languages. I believe that, even though I do not have a lot of experience as a software engineer, I could be the person you are looking for.
